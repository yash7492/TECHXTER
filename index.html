About Us

Techxter is the annual national level paper presentation competition organized by the IETE Students' Forum of SIES Graduate School of Technology. Every year participants from across the country enroll and present their innovative ideas on this platform. This year, we are organising the 8th iteration of Techxter, and it only gets better each year. Bridge the gap between innovation and the human mind with careful dissection of facts and ideas. Unleash your technical and presentation skills on this platform of wisdom.

FAQ

1. The length of the abstract should not exceed 500 words.

2. It must include the Name, Affiliation, E-mail ID and Mobile number of the first author just below the title on the first page followed by the abstract.

3. Names of all other participants must also be included.

4. Participants must also mention subject area upon which the subject topic is based.

5. For the selected abstracts, author must submit the full length paper in a maximum of four A4 size pages in IEEE two-column format.

1. The participating team should upload their abstract to techxter.in.

2. If the abstract is shortlisted, the event heads will contact the concerned team.

3. The team will then have to make a payment of rs700/- by Demand Draft.

4. Please note that the above mentioned payment is for a maximum of 3 team members.

5. The team will have to pay an additional amount of 100/- for every extra member that they wish to include in their team.

6. The team will also have to mail their entire paper to the above mentioned email ID after the selection process.
